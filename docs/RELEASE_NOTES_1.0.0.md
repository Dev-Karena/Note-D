# NoteD Version 1.0.0 — Official Release Notes

We are thrilled to announce the official release of **NoteD Version 1.0.0**! Engineered by **DEV KARENA**, NoteD is a highly secure, offline-first personal notes and category management application built to give you absolute control over your private information. 

NoteD runs 100% locally on your device. Without cloud servers, advertising algorithms, or tracking libraries, your journals, checklists, ideas, and passwords remain secure and private.

---

## 🌟 What's New in Version 1.0.0

This release introduces standard features designed around three core principles: **absolute privacy**, **on-device reliability**, and a **visually polished, modern Material 3 interface**.

---

## 🛠️ Key Features

*   **Dynamic Notes Workspace:** Create, edit, search, and delete your notes. Personalize your visual organization by picking from vibrant custom background card color palettes.
*   **Custom Folders & Tag Filters:** Group your ideas on the fly! Design custom folders (e.g., *Work*, *Personal*, *Creative*, *Finance*) with custom tags and filter your notes list instantly with a single tap.
*   **Smart Pinning:** Pin your most critical notes to the top of the interface, keeping them visible above other historical entries.
*   **Decluttered Archiving:** Soft-archive items to hide completed tasks or private logs from the active dashboard feed without deleting your data permanently.

---

## 🔐 Advanced Security Enhancements

NoteD features on-device encryption systems to protect your data from unauthorized access:

*   **Hardware Biometrics Entry Gate:** Lock access to your workspace. Supports fingerprint or face sensors, requiring user verification on app launch and resume.
*   **Custom PIN Lock:** Secures access with an independent, safe 4-digit numeric PIN, using secure hashing math to protect configuration files.
*   **Secure Elements (hardware) sandboxing:** System settings, state variables, and lock parameters are encrypted inside specialized, hardware-protected storage (AES-256 GCM) on your phone.

---

## 🚀 Productivity Improvements

*   **High-Reliability Alarms & Reminders:** Set custom calendar alarms (Daily, Weekly, Monthly repetitions). NoteD handles background tasks gracefully to prevent delayed alerts, and alarms automatically restore if your device reboots.
*   **Elastic Instant Search:** Find your notes instantly. A real-time database indexing pipeline searches titles, tags, and content bodies as you type.
*   **Accessible Material 3 Design:** Includes native accessibility support (high-contrast ratios, large touch targets, dynamic font scaling) and adaptive Dark Theme support to reduce eye strain in low-light environments.

---

## ⚠️ Known Limitations

*   **No Cross-Device Cloud Syncing:** Because NoteD operates complete offline isolation for absolute privacy, notes cannot automatically sync across multiple physical phones. Export/Import mechanisms will be added in upcoming updates.
*   **Lost PIN Recovery:** For security reasons, if you activate PIN security, lose your PIN, and have biometrics turned off, **the developer cannot recover or reset your PIN**. Please choose a PIN you will remember.
